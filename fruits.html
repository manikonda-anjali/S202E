Fruits are the edible, often sweet and juicy parts of plants that usually contain seeds.

They come in a wide variety of colors, shapes, and flavors, such as apples, bananas, oranges, and berries.

Rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, fruits are an important part of a healthy diet.

They can be eaten fresh, dried, juiced, or used in cooking and baking. Regular fruit consumption supports overall health and helps reduce the risk of many chronic diseases.
